Collaborative Research Data Management Support for FEdA projects#

Linares, Jimena, Ebert, Barbara, Eberhardt, Jan, Frohne, Katharina, Sauerland, Kristin, Mozygemba, Kati, Miller, Bernhard

Published 2023-02-09

Licensed cc-by-4.0

The poster has been created for and shown on the first european conference of the BMBF Research Initiative for the Conservation of Biodiversity (FEdA) „Biodiversity and Human Well-Being – Europe’s Role in Shaping Our Future“ from November 9th - 11th, 2022.

The National Research Data Infrastructure (NFDI) and FEdA started at about the same time in 2020. Given the importance attributed to the availability of FEdA research outputs, including data, the speakers of the two initiatives quickly decided to join forces. A FEdA data policy was established for the projects and a support workflow was set up. The FEdA Coordination Office is the initial point of contact, with data experts from NFDI in the background for requests regarding specific data types and community standards.

TA2M1 - Collaboration with related NFDI consortia#

Sauerland, Kristin, Ebert, Barbara, Güntsch, Anton

Published 2023-01-30

Licensed cc-by-4.0

EN

At the NFDI4Biodiversity All Hands Conference, October 12-14, 2022, Task Area 2 Measure 1 (TA2M1) gave an overview of its activities, which are dedicated to NFDI4Biodiversity’s involvement within the NFDI e.V. as a whole, as well as to the cooperation with individual NFDI consortia. NFDI4Biodiversity is intensely engaged in the four sections and the two task forces of the NFDI e.V. that deal with cross-cutting topics relevant to all NFDI consortia. Moreover, NFDI4Biodiversity, in cooperation with other NFDI consortia and RDM-initatives, frequently offers training and events. Finally, the implementation and strengthening of multidisciplinary Use Cases is another essential level of collaboration with other NFDI consortia.

 

DE

Auf der NFDI4Biodiversity All Hands Conference vom 12. bis 14. Oktober 2022, gab die Task Area 2 Measure 1 (TA2M1) einen Überblick über ihre Aktivitäten, die sich dem  Engagement von NFDI4Biodiversity innerhalb des NFDI e.V., als auch der Kooperationen mit einzelnen NFDI-Konsortien widmen. NFDI4Biodiversity engagiert sich intensiv in den vier Sektionen und den beiden Task Forces des NFDI e.V., die sich mit Querschnittsthemen beschäftigen, die für alle NFDI-Konsortien relevant sind. Darüber hinaus bietet NFDI4Biodiversity in Zusammenarbeit mit anderen NFDI-Konsortien und RDM-Initiativen regelmäßig Trainings und Veranstaltungen an. Schließlich ist die Implementierung und Stärkung von multidisziplinären Use Cases eine weitere wesentliche Ebene in der Zusammenarbeit mit anderen NFDI-Konsortien.

TA2M2: (Inter)National Networking#

Fichtmueller, David, Engel, Judith, Sauerland, Kristin, Luther, Katja, Güntsch, Anton

Published 2024-04-25

Licensed cc-by-4.0

This poster describes the national and international networking activities of the NFDI4Biodiversity project as documented by the Measure 2 Task 2. The poster shows which projects, initiatives and organizations are relevant partners and planned future activities of the task. It was presented during the poster session at the NFDI4Biodiversity All Hands Conference on 2024-04-25 in Munich.
